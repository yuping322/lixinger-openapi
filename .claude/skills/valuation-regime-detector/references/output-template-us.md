### 3) Interpretation

#### 3.1 Historical context

- How unusual the current multiple is vs recent history.
- Whether the asset is near a regime extreme or closer to normal.
- Whether multiple expansion/contraction already appears stretched.

#### 3.2 Relative context

- Why the current premium or discount might be deserved.
- Whether peers are also at extreme valuations.
- Which relative metric matters most for this asset class or industry.

#### 3.3 What changes the regime

- Earnings acceleration, rate moves, policy changes, or narrative shifts.
- What would justify further premium expansion.
- What would turn the current setup into a de-rating risk.
