### 3) Interpretation

#### 3.1 What changed fundamentally

- Was the surprise mostly in top-line, margin, or forward guidance.

#### 3.2 What price action is saying

- Was the opening gap confirmed by intraday follow-through.

- Is post-earnings drift likely if revisions continue.

- Which 2–3 variables deserve the closest watch after the report.
