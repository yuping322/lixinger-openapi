### 3) Interpretation

#### 3.1 How crowded is it

- Explain whether crowding is broad-based or concentrated in a handful of names.

#### 3.2 What could trigger rotation

#### 3.3 Portfolio implications

| Risk | Trigger | Monitor | Mitigation / what changes the view |
