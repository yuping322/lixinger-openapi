Structured format for presenting insider trading analysis results.

Use this structure for each identified company:

#### Historical Impact of Insider Buying

[Analysis of how previous insider buying clusters in this specific stock have preceded price performance:]

- **Prior cluster [date range]**: [N] insiders bought $[amount]. Stock performance over subsequent 3/6/12 months: [+/- X%]
- **Prior cluster [date range]**: [N] insiders bought $[amount]. Stock performance over subsequent 3/6/12 months: [+/- X%]
- **Historical hit rate**: [X] of [Y] prior insider buying clusters were followed by positive 12-month returns

If no prior cluster history is available, note that and reference broader academic research on insider buying alpha.

#### Management Confidence Signal

[2–4 bullet points interpreting what the buying pattern signals:]

- What the buying likely indicates about management's view of current valuation
- Any known upcoming catalysts (earnings, product launches, regulatory milestones) that insiders may be positioning ahead of
- Whether the buying pattern is contrarian (into weakness) or confirmatory (into strength)
- Significance of the specific insiders involved (e.g., CFO buying is a stronger financial signal)

#### Potential Red Flags

[2–4 bullet points covering concerns that temper the bullish signal:]

- Any simultaneous insider selling activity
- Structural concerns (contractual buy obligations, signaling buys, etc.)
- Company-level risks (litigation, regulatory, financial distress)
- Sector or macro headwinds that insiders may be underestimating
